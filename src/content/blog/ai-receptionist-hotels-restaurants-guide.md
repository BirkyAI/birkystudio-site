Running a hotel or restaurant means you're always "on." Guests call at midnight asking about availability. Walk-ins want to know your specials. Someone needs directions while you're in the middle of a dinner rush. An AI receptionist handles all of this for you, and it never takes a break.

## What Is an AI Receptionist?

Think of it as a virtual front desk staff member. It answers phone calls, responds to messages, handles reservations, and answers common questions. The difference is that it works 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and it speaks multiple languages.

Instead of missing calls when you're busy (or asleep), every inquiry gets an immediate, helpful response. For hotels, that means booking requests handled at 2 AM. For restaurants, that means table reservations confirmed during the dinner rush without pulling your host away from the door.

## Why Hotels and Restaurants Need This

Hospitality businesses face a unique challenge: customers expect instant, personal service, but staffing a front desk or phone line around the clock is expensive. Here's what an AI receptionist solves:

**No missed calls.** Studies show that 85% of callers who reach voicemail won't leave a message. They'll just call the next place. An AI receptionist picks up every single time.

**Multilingual support.** If your hotel or restaurant serves tourists, you need to communicate in English, Spanish, and possibly other languages. An AI receptionist handles this automatically without hiring bilingual staff.

**No hiring headaches.** Finding reliable front desk staff or hosts is one of the biggest challenges in hospitality. An AI receptionist doesn't call in sick, doesn't need training every few months, and doesn't require a salary.

**Consistent quality.** Every guest gets the same friendly, professional experience. No bad days, no attitude, no forgetting to mention the daily special.

## What Can It Actually Do?

For hotels:
- Answer questions about room availability, amenities, and pricing
- Process reservation requests and send confirmations
- Provide check-in/check-out times and directions
- Handle common requests (late checkout, extra towels, parking)

For restaurants:
- Take reservation requests by phone, WhatsApp, or Telegram
- Answer questions about the menu, hours, and location
- Share daily specials and promotional offers
- Handle large party bookings and dietary inquiries
